Commercial Performance & Profitability Analytics for a European FMCG Brand

Conducted an end-to-end commercial performance and profitability analysis for Choco de Luxe, a European FMCG brand, to address the lack of a unified performance view across markets. Analysed £5.32M in revenue across countries, sales channels, products, and sales teams using Tableau. Identified delivery inefficiencies (93% success rate in key markets), underperforming channels and product segments, and significant sales performance gaps against a €100K profit target. Delivered actionable recommendations to improve logistics efficiency, optimise channel profitability, increase focus on high-margin products, and enhance sales productivity.

St. Augustine General Hospital: Operations & Patient Outcomes Analytics

Designed and implemented an end-to-end healthcare analytics solution to improve visibility into hospital operations and patient outcomes. The project transformed unstructured hospital data into a scalable star schema model and delivered interactive Power BI dashboards to monitor key metrics such as patient flow, departmental capacity, treatment effectiveness, and workforce performance. The solution enables stakeholders to identify operational inefficiencies, reduce readmissions, and optimise resource allocation through data-driven decision-making.

Customer Churn and Retention Analytics for a UK-Based Multinational Retail Bank

Built an end-to-end churn intelligence solution for a UK retail bank by transforming fragmented customer data into a structured analytics model using SQL and delivering an interactive dashboard in Microsoft Power BI, addressing the lack of visibility into churn drivers and at-risk segments. Analysed a 10,000-customer portfolio (£271M revenue) to uncover key drivers such as inactivity, low engagement, and regional disparities, and developed risk-based segmentation to enable targeted retention strategies. This approach supports proactive churn reduction, improved customer lifetime value, and smarter allocation of retention efforts toward high-value customers. .
